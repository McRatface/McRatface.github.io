About the Band

The Mountain Goats are an American indie folk band formed in Claremont, California in 1991. The band is led by singer-songwriter John Darnielle and currently consists of Darnielle, Peter Hughes, and Jon Wurster. The band has released over twenty studio albums and is known for their unique blend of acoustic guitar and autobiographical lyrics.
